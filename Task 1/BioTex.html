Active Pharma

Pharma Corporation Specilized in Diseases

What We Do

Biotex is a premier product development accelerator for new medical technology. In addition to our own product portfolio,
we partner with entrepreneurs and early stage companies to provide product development, intellectual property guidance, regulatory strategy, and commercial manufacturing.
Our team of designers and engineers transform ideas into incredible products that look and work fantastic!

OUR TEAM

Our people are our greatest assets. Each of our team members is a vital part of our company's vision to fuel innovation in medical technologies.

FACILITY

At our new Houston Facility, we are equipped with two stories of office, lab, and manufacturing space. We have a Class 7 cleanroom, a Mechanical workshop and makers space, electrical labs, and office space set aside for some of our company partners.

LEADERSHIP

Excellence starts at the top. Our top leadership team at Biotex has laid a foundation for growth and development for all team members as well as for the company.

RESEARCH & STRATEGY

From initial problem solving to successful commercialization, medical device research requires navigation of numerous factors

Our Features

    Biotex is an FDA-registered and ISO 13485:2016 certified medical device manufacturer and can serve as a contract manufacturing partner

    Our experienced team includes engineers with expertise in industrial design, mechanical, electrical, optics, firmware, and software (mobile & desktop) development.
